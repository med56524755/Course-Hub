How to Add Course Hub Shortcuts to Your iOS Home Screen

If you use the Safari browser on an iOS device, it's easy to create shortcuts on the Home screen that open directly to your favorite websites. Here's how to create Home screen website shortcuts:

  1. Launch Safari and navigate to a website you frequently visit.
  2. Tap the Bookmark icon at the bottom of the screen (it resembles a box with an upward-pointing arrow).
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Enter a Name for the shortcut (you can accept the suggested name or enter a custom one), then tap Add to save the new shortcut icon to the home screen.
  5. The new icon will appear next to your other app icons. If needed, scroll through the pages to find it. To use the icon, tap it to go directly to the saved website in Safari.

The instructions above apply to all iOS devices, including iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch.
